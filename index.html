At Sherwood Dental Cosmetic and Family Dentistry, we understand that a healthy smile boosts a person’s confidence and self-worth. Our dental office is committed to offering you only the highest quality dental care.Our services encompass a full suite of dental care options, including examinations, teeth cleaning, teeth straightening, and more. We firmly believe that maintaining the excellent condition of the teeth, as well as correcting any flaws, is essential to a patient’s quality of life. A beautiful set of pearly whites can have a profound effect on a person’s overall appearance and confidence. Our dental office is proud to offer complete cosmetic dental services. Through our advanced dental cleaning, restoration, and protection techniques, white, straight, and beautiful teeth are within reach.Sherwood Dental is equipped with a complete suite of dental equipment, tools, and expertise to handle any dental procedure professionally. From teeth extractions to root canal therapy, we are at your service.We are particularly proud of our dental implants, which provide patients with a permanent, durable, and, most of all, beautiful replacements to the teeth they lost.In terms of payment, we offer Direct Insurance Billing. This arrangement allows us to submit the patient’s dental insurance claim directly to the company. The insurance company will then pay us directly, thus saving patients a lot of time and worry.
